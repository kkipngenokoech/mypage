Hi, I’m at PhD student at UCL$^*$ studying theoretical neuroscience and machine learning.

My research is in spatial navigation, reinforcement learning and representation learning – I use theoretical methods and data-driven techniques from machine learning to understand biological intelligence (NeuroAI) under the supervision of Kimberly Stachenfeld (Google DeepMind), Claudia Clopath (Imperial) and Caswell Barry (UCL).

I build and maintain open-source tools for science including the RatInABox package. I also co-founded CaMinA, a summer school for computational neuroscience and machine learning in Africa.
